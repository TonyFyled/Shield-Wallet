Next up   |         OVERVIEW

OVERVIEW

Shield Wallet is a multi-chain crypto wallet app that helps you manage your cryptocurrency investments. With the wallet app, you can easily aggregate multiple blockchain tokens into one convenient location, giving you full control over your crypto assets.

Hinged on the Proof-of-Stake (POS) consensus, the wallet app gives you the flexibility of staking your tokens native to POS blockchains like Cardano (ADA), Solana (SOL).

Next up   |         PROBLEM STATEMENT

Problem Statement

Managing multiple blockchain accounts and keeping track of various cryptocurrency investments can be challenging, especially for new users. Many current crypto wallets only support a single blockchain or lack advanced features such as staking, making it difficult for users to fully maximize their investments and earning potential.

The solution

Shield wallet proffers a seamless crypto wallet app that explores both the multi-chain tendencies of a conventional crypto wallet and the staking mechanism of a Proof-of-Stake (PoS) blockchain.

Next up   |         MARKET RESEARCH

Market Research

The claim

The growth of crypto staking has been significant in recent years. Proof of Stake (PoS) consensus mechanism became more and more popular, as it offers several advantages over the traditional Proof of Work (PoW) mechanism. This has led to an increase in the number of PoS blockchain networks and the number of digital assets that can be staked.

The problem

Managing multiple blockchain assets and keeping track of various cryptocurrency investments can be time-consuming and confusing for users (especially new users).

Many current crypto wallet solutions only support a single blockchain or lack advanced features such as staking, making it difficult for users to fully maximize their investments and earning potential.

Next up   |         COMPETITIVE ANALYSIS

Competitive Analysis

I sampled two wallets Trust wallet and Phantom wallet. I compared the staking experience of each app as a new and returning user. I also sampled comments on the App store to get the essence of what their users struggle with the most while using them and what they are not too keen on.

TrustWallet App

STRENGTHS

Well designed app with a simple, modern and bold texts and colors with high contrast.

Beginner friendly UI, easy to navigate

Supports many cryptocurrencies

WEAKNESSES

No in-app staking feature for PoS assets

DApps function for IOS seems to omitted. Would require external tooling.

Notifications for some PoS assets blocked

Phantom Wallet App

STRENGTHS

The app has a user-friendly interface that is easy to navigate and understand, even for new users.

The app allows users to stake their assets, providing them with an opportunity to earn passive income.

WEAKNESSES

The app may not support as many cryptocurrencies as some of the competitors.

Some users may find the app too complex to use, especially if they are not familiar with cryptocurrency and blockchain technology.

The icons in the bottom navigation don’t have labels on them.
